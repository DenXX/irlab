(L006) It gets 3.9 inches. More carnivorous than herbivore, but no issue there. Would these two be... show more My rainbow shark is getting bullied in the 55 gallon by the Firemouth.

So, I am going to try and get a 30-38 gallon tank started for him.

He is currently 5".
The pleco species is Brown Dot Peckoltia. (L006)
It gets 3.9 inches. More carnivorous than herbivore, but no issue there.

Would these two be okay in a 30-38 gallon?
They'd have an Aqueon Quiet Flow 30 filter. Also more likely sand substrate, a lot of driftwood and plants. Not really intending for other fish in the tank, just these two.
Update: A heated tank is a definite thing. I'm hoping to get 2.3 WPG as well...
Update 2: A L006 pleco does NOT get over a foot. They get 4-5 inches on average.
The rainbow shark gets 6 inches mostly and this one is full grown right now. Not growing too much except in the belly.
